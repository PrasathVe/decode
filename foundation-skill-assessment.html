What Is a Foundation Skill Assessment?

A foundation skill assessment is a structured evaluation designed to identify an individual’s natural strengths, problem-solving abilities, communication patterns, analytical thinking, creativity, and learning behavior.

This assessment helps students and professionals gain clarity before making major educational or career decisions.
